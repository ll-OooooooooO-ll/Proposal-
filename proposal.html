The Moment I Found the Right Words.


Hey dear… remember when I told you I had something to say? Back then, I didn’t really have the words. I just knew that you mattered more to me than I could explain, and I wanted a reason to stay closer to you, even if it meant holding on to a little mystery.

Days passed, and you kept asking me about it again and again. Every time, I held back — not because I didn’t care, but because the truth was still growing inside me. I told you I was waiting for the right time, and that wasn’t just an excuse. And now, I finally understand why I couldn't say it then. What I wanted to tell you wasn’t just a sentence — it was a feeling, a quiet emotion that kept growing stronger every time we talked, every time you smile, every time you simply existed in my world.



So here it is… I like you. Not just as a friend. Not just because you're kind, smart, or beautiful. I like you—the way you make the ordinary feel special, the way you unknowingly became the most important part of my days. And today, I finally want to say it properly.






Will you be mine?









But if you don’t feel the same, that’s completely okay. Let’s just pretend this message never happened—and keep everything between us just the way it was, with no awkwardness at all.
